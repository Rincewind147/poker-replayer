Battle for Poker AI Supremacy

Write a bot to compete in real-time Texas Hold'em against other AIs. Go all-in, win chips and become the nerd king.

How it works

You write a poker-playing computer program
Our network client connects your program to the casino and join matches
The server keeps your bot up to date and asks your bot for actions
Your bot has up to five seconds to respond with your move
Play continues bot-on-bot until only one of you has chips left
After the match, you can download log files and analyze your performance
